Site inspire

Lusion is a creative production studio that creates visually stunning content utilising cutting-edge technology and techniques to bring ideas to life. The challenge was to undertake a complete rebranding of the company and create a new website that showcased their exceptional capabilities.

The rebranding process involved distilling the essence of Lusion into a clean and recognisable logo, formed by just five pixels.

This minimalist, striking emblem symbolises Lusion's ability to create something extraordinary from the simplest elements.

The creation of the new Lusion website employed a brand new approach, seamlessly integrating Lusion's expertise into every section without compromising the user experience.
